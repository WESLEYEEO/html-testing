My Top Animes of all Time

  1. Demon Slayer

    • Best scripted; Gradual intoduction of their big guns
      It is also very cultured and well-presented.

  2. Jijutsu Kaisen

    • Great action; Real show of power and scorery
      Run away from this movie if you aren't a fan of mass destruction.

  3. Classroom of the Elite

    • Real show of intelligence; A show of action, thriller, and show-biz suspense all in one anime
      If you like this one, then your IQ is way higher than you actually rate it.

  4. Promise Neverland

    • Great thriller series with loads of twists and suspense
      Leave this one if you get easily irriated when children are hurt.

  5. Avatar: The Last Airbender

    • This series never gets old. The writer had the intention of giving the viewer something for their whole family
      p.s. I'm rewatching it in 2024!!
